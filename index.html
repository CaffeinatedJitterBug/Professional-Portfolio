Farming Simulator

View Website

GitHub Repository

Farming Simulator is a game about growing and selling crops. Every player starts with one plot to grow crops on. Harvested crops can be sold to the market which has ever changing prices based off of the real life stock market. Players can buy more plots as well as other upgrades with the money they make selling their crops.
